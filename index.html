FAQ

Your questions, answered

How much do dental treatments cost?

Costs vary by treatment and complexity. We offer transparent, upfront pricing and flexible 0% EMI options. Routine check-ups start affordably, and we always share full costs before any procedure begins — no hidden charges.

How do I book an appointment?

Book online using the appointment form above, tap "Call Now", or message us on WhatsApp. We offer same-day appointments subject to availability and confirm every request within 20 minutes during clinic hours.

Do you support dental insurance?

Yes. We accept most major dental insurance plans and assist with cashless claims and paperwork wherever applicable. Bring your insurance details and our front-desk team will guide you.

Do you provide emergency dental care?

Absolutely. We provide same-day emergency care for severe pain, swelling, trauma and broken teeth. Call the clinic immediately and we'll prioritise you.

Are treatments painful?

We specialise in gentle, minimally-invasive dentistry with effective local anaesthesia and modern technology, so most patients feel little to no discomfort during and after treatment.
